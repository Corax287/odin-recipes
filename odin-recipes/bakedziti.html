Baked Ziti

Description

ziti pasta (a smooth tube-shaped pasta), red sauce, and a mix of cheeses (ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan)

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 6 ounces of provolone cheese
  • 6 ounces of mozarella cheese
  • 1.5 cups of sour cream

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Cook ziti pasta
  3. Cook ground beef,onion and spaghetti
  4. Spread half of the ziti pasta in the bottom of dish
  5. Top with provolone cheese
  6. Top with sour cream
  7. add half of meat sauce
  8. Bake for 30 minutes
