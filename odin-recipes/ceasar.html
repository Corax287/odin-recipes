Ceasar salad recipe

Description

Delightful salad with a mix of legumes,vegetables and cooked chicken

Ingredients

  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Mushrooms
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh chicken breast
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Lemons

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Cook Chicken breast
  3. Chop iceberg,shrooms and cherrry tomatoes
  4. Mix vegetables in bowl with squeezed lemon juice
  5. Add cooked chicken
  6. Mix ingredients
  7. Serve with balsamic vinegar topping
